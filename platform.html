Trading Methods

English Auction
Lowest start price → highest bidder wins. Min 5% bid increment. Auto-extend 10 min on bids in last 5 min. Forfeit after 24h non-payment.
Fixed Price
Instant purchase at seller-set price. Auto 50% fee discount when paying with $SOVE. Price changes possible while keeping existing offers.
Make Offer
Buyer proposes desired price. Seller accept/reject/counter. Offer validity: 24h / 48h / 7d.
OTC (Over-the-Counter)
$100K+ bulk trades only. Escrow smart-contract for safe settlement. Mutually agreed price. Enhanced KYC required.

Anti-Scalping 5-Layer Mechanism

Price Cap
Promoter sets resale max (e.g., 150% of original). Contracts reject trades above the cap.
GIWA ID Binding
SBT linked at purchase, enforces 1-person N-ticket limit. Both transferor and receiver must pass KYC.
Transfer Lock
Transfers/resales blocked starting 48h before event. Enforced at contract level — cannot be bypassed.
Check-in Identity Verification
QR scan + GIWA ID match check. Both must match for entry.
Auto Royalty
2% auto-distributed to the artist on every resale. Scalping behavior is converted into artist revenue.
